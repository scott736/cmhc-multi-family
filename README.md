# CMHC Multi-Family

The definitive guide to Canadian CMHC multi-family financing — programs, calculators, underwriting logic, lender landscape, developer pathways, policy tracker, and data sources.

**Current to April 2026.** Incorporates the major July 14, 2025 premium overhaul and November 2024 policy tightening.

## What's here

- **Programs** — MLI Select, MLI Standard, ACLP, AHF, CHDP, plus specialized (retirement, student, supportive, SRO). Side-by-side comparison matrix.
- **Calculators** — MLI Select point scorer, triple-constrained loan sizer, post-July-2025 premium calculator, cash-flow projection, scenario comparison, premium credit calculator.
- **Underwriting** — DCR and loan sizing, LTV and value determination, affordability thresholds by CMA, opex/vacancy/reserve benchmarks.
- **Lenders** — top 5 by insured MUA, monolines vs. life cos vs. banks vs. credit unions, how deals actually get done.
- **Developers** — construction-to-term vs. completion takeout, how 95% LTC + 50yr amort transforms returns, municipal incentive stacking (Toronto, Vancouver, Calgary, Edmonton, Ottawa, Montreal), provincial rent rules.
- **Policy** — maintained timeline of every CMHC change since June 2024.
- **Data** — Rental Market Survey, HMIP, StatCan Table 34-10-0133-01, `cmhc` R package, Open Government Portal.
